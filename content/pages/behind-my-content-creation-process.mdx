Before publishing a comparison or article on my website, I follow a rigorous method combining **research, SEO strategy, and optimized writing**. My goal is simple: to offer useful, well-structured, and perfectly **optimized** content to help users make the best choice. Here is my step-by-step process, from keyword research to illustration and publishing.

### **Trend Analysis and Keyword Research**

To begin writing, I make sure the topic aligns with real user searches. I start by typing a relevant query on Google, based on the search habits of my audience. Since my site focuses on baby products, I look for expressions specific to this field.

For example, for my article on the **Kinderkraft car seat**, I start with a generic query and scroll to the **"Related Searches"**. These suggestions help me identify the exact terms users are looking for. I then select and slightly adapt one of these queries to maximize impact while keeping it natural.

I then analyze keyword authority using **SEMrush**. My goal is to find keywords with the **lowest SEO difficulty** while maintaining a good search volume. This increases the chances of ranking on the first page of search results.

For instance, the keyword group **"Kinderkraft car seat"** has a **Keyword Difficulty (KD) of 19**, indicating low SEO competition. In other words, it is relatively **easy to rank on the first page of Google** with this query. This type of keyword is a **strategic opportunity** to maximize visibility while addressing user intent.

I also use **Google Trends** to check whether a keyword maintains **consistent interest throughout the year** or is **seasonal**. This step helps me prioritize keywords with **long-term traffic potential**.

### **Choosing the Title and Competitor Analysis**

After selecting the right keyword, I create an **optimized title**. It must **naturally include the main keyword** while being **catchy and attractive** to draw attention. The goal is to **increase the click-through rate (CTR)** and drive traffic.

For example, I used the title: **"Kinderkraft SAFETY-FIX Car Seat: Review, Test, Price & Deals 2025"**. This title includes the **main keyword**, uses *high-search terms* (**Review, Test, Price, Deals**), and adds **recency** (*2025*) to increase clicks.

As part of the competitive analysis, I review at least five well-ranked articles. I also use tools like **Quillbot** to estimate **word count**. The goal is to create an article that is **longer, more complete, and more informative** than the competition, increasing my chances of ranking on the first page.

### **Information Collection and Analysis**

Before writing, I gather **reliable and diversified information** to ensure the article is credible and relevant. This deep research includes:

 	- **Reading competitor articles**: Helps identify common points, recurring angles, strengths/weaknesses, and what is often missing. This allows me to create a **more complete and value-added article**.
 	- **Visiting the product manufacturer’s official site**: I collect technical specifications, marketing claims, and official visuals to **ensure accuracy**.
 	- **Analyzing customer reviews on Amazon and other e-commerce sites**: Real user feedback highlights the **pros and cons** of a product, helping to build a **balanced and credible review**.

### **SEO-Optimized Writing**

Once research is complete, I start writing while applying **on-page SEO best practices** to maximize visibility.

 	- **Strategic integration of the main keyword**: I include the keyword in the **title, H1 tag,** and the **first sentence of the introduction** to help Google quickly understand the topic.

 	- **Using 1.fr for semantic analysis**: This tool helps me find **related secondary keywords** to enrich the article’s semantic field and improve its chances of ranking for related queries.

### **Plagiarism and Quality Check**

Once the article is written, I do a **final quality check** to ensure the content is **unique, optimized, and smooth**.

 	- **Plagiarism check with Quetext**: I ensure the **plagiarism rate is below 10%** to maintain originality.

 	- **Final SEO optimization with Yoast SEO**: This WordPress plugin allows **real-time SEO analysis** for readability, keyword density, internal linking, HTML tags, etc.
 	- **Proofreading and correction**: I do a final **deep review** to correct any **spelling, grammar, or syntax errors** and ensure **smooth readability**.

### **Image Insertion and Product Link Integration**

Once the content is finalized and optimized, I move to **visual and commercial formatting** to enhance user experience and conversion potential.

**Selecting relevant illustrative images**

I carefully choose images directly related to the topic, such as:

 	- **Product photos** (e.g., Kinderkraft SAFETY-FIX car seat)
 	- **Usage scenes**: e.g., a child properly seated in the car seat
 	- **Educational visuals**: diagrams, comparisons, etc.

The goal is to **make the article more visually appealing** and help readers better understand the product and its use.

**Inserting links to mentioned products**

I strategically place **links to product pages** (Amazon or other e-commerce platforms), typically:

 	- **Under product images**
 	- **At the end of product descriptions**
 	- **In comparison tables**

This improves both **navigation** and the **conversion rate**.
