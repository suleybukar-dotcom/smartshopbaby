Since 1950, Mustela baby has been dedicated to supporting parents with gentle, natural skincare products for babies and the whole family. As a proud French family brand certified B Corp, Mustela combines innovation with environmental responsibility. With recognized expertise in neonatal dermatology, the brand offers solutions tailored to the specific needs of young skin while promoting responsible sourcing and sustainable practices. Let’s explore the history, commitments, and values that make Mustela a leader in children’s skincare.

## Mustela Baby: 70 Years of Commitment to Natural Baby Care and Family Wellness

### **History and Origins of Mustela Baby**

Mustela was founded in 1950 by pharmacist Claude Guillon and entrepreneur Paul Berthomé, who created the first cleansing milk specifically for babies. Over the past 70 years, Mustela has grown into a trusted brand for families, focusing on gentle, effective skincare solutions.

### **Brand Commitment **

Mustela is committed to being a responsible brand, certified as a B Corp. This certification reflects their dedication to social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency. The brand aims not only to be the best in the world but the best for the world.

### **Product Range Mustela Baby**

Mustela offers a wide variety of skincare products for newborns and children, addressing common skin concerns such as diaper rash, dryness, and irritation. The formulations are designed to be safe and effective, using natural ingredients.

### **Natural Ingredients in Mustela Baby Products**

The brand prioritizes sourcing high-quality natural and organic ingredients. For example, key ingredients like avocado are responsibly sourced through ethical trade practices, ensuring sustainability and respect for biodiversity.

### **Sustainability Initiatives of Mustela Baby**

Mustela is dedicated to minimizing its environmental impact through several initiatives:

 	- **Eco-Conception:** Mustela baby products are designed with sustainability in mind, using recyclable packaging and minimizing material use.
 	- **Responsible Sourcing:** Ingredients are selected from ethical suppliers, ensuring a positive impact on communities and the environment.
 	- **Waste Reduction:** Mustela repurposes ingredients that would otherwise be discarded, transforming them into valuable cosmetic components.

### **Research and Innovation **

Mustela invests in ambitious research programs to understand the unique physiology of baby and children’s skin. This expertise allows the brand to develop effective formulations that cater specifically to young skin.

### **Community Support for Mustela Baby**

The brand is committed to supporting projects that improve the quality of life for children. This includes initiatives aimed at ensuring children's well-being and fostering healthier environments.

### **Certifications and Recognition **

Mustela has received the B Corp certification, which recognizes companies for their triple bottom line performance—economic, social, and environmental. This commitment to excellence reinforces Mustela’s reputation as a leader in the skincare industry.

### **Customer Trust in Mustela Baby**

Generations of parents have relied on Mustela baby products for their family’s skincare needs. The brand’s long-standing reputation for quality and safety continues to build trust among new parents.

### **Future Goals for Mustela Baby**

Mustela aims to contribute to global climate goals by 2050, focusing on sustainable practices in sourcing, production, and packaging. Their commitment to renewable energy and reduced resource consumption underlines their dedication to a healthier planet.
